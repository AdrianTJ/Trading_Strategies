# Plan 04-01: Backend Metrics & Synthetic Benchmarks

## Objective

This plan upgrades the backend to support institutional-grade risk metrics and generates standard benchmarks for comparison. We will refine the Sharpe and Sortino ratio calculations and implement a synthetic 60/40 (Equity/Bond) portfolio benchmark by approximating bond price movements from 10Y Treasury yields.

## Context

Current analytics use a simplified Sortino ratio (std of negative returns) which is mathematically incorrect for financial benchmarking. We also lack a way to distinguish "benchmarks" from "user strategies" in the database. 

Research from `04-RESEARCH.md` provides the specific formulas for Downside Deviation and Bond returns.
